Research Projects

EEG / fMRI to Image Reconstruction

I investigate how visual information can be reconstructed from neural signals under severe information constraints. My work uses EEG and fMRI datasets (e.g., THINGS, NSD) and frames reconstruction as a probabilistic inference problem.

I design diffusion-based generative pipelines to model uncertainty, noise, and information bottlenecks, and explore real-time EEG-to-image reconstruction under latency and robustness constraints.
